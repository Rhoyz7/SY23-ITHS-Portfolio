ZooQuarium

Business

Animals from both the land and the sea can be found at ZooQuarium, a park. The animals that are held at zoos are either rescued and waiting to be released into the wild, crippled and unable to return to the wild and endangered species. The zoo houses giraffes, elephants, lions, orcas, manta rays, sharks, and many other animals.

History

The ZooQuarium, also referred to as ZQ, is situated in the center of Dallas. On April 28, 2023, the zoo officially opened to the public. Since then, it has grown to become one of the top zoological parks in the world. ZQ was created after extensive discussion and planning. A flock of flamingos were housed as the zoo's initial inhabitants. The ZQ has evolved and extended over time, and new displays and ecosystems are frequently introduced.
